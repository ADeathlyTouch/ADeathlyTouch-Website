Welcome!

YouTuber, Streamer, and Developer.

Welcome to my world!

Hello there I am A Deathly Touch, although I usually go by Deathly. I live in Wisconsin, US and I am 19 years old. I like to play Call of Duty, Minecraft, Modded Minecraft, and much, much more. I work 40 hours a week and can only record on the weekends. Recently though I began doing many more things. I joined the Minecraft Modding team know as TheDragonTeam©. I am also part of Team Avion©, another Minecraft Modding team. Recently my friend TJKenMate and I began production of a new Minecraft Modpack called Avion. It is a Modpack based on a continuous progression system and the need for users to think creatively to design unique and innovative ways to create the perfect world of automation. We have both put countless hours into ensure that Avion will be perfect and we hope to have it released very soon.

Avion

Avion is a modpack designed to make you think. It will challenge you to use unique and innovative ways to make your base's automation perfect. With a continuous progression system you'll always be striving to go further and further into the pack until you make the perfect world. With 2 separate modes including, Avion the challenging mode, Classic the default mode. Our Avion mode will adjust recipes and add a progression system akin to that of FTB Infinity Evolved Expert mode. Classic mode will still have some altered recipes and the progression system but won't be nearly as difficult. These are both subject to change at any time. The full mod list has not been completed yet but we expect to have over 100 mods all handpicked and adjusted to the thesis of Avion.

Teams

I am a part of two separate teams. Team Avion, and The Dragon Team. Both are Minecraft Modding Teams and in both we develop new mods for Minecraft. Team Avion is currently developing Tesla Essentials, a mod that adds a set of essential tools and blocks using the Tesla API. The Dragon Team has numerous projects and mods including a widely known mod, Armor Plus, a mod based on exploration, killing, building, getting geared up, fight the bosses and explore the depths of your worlds.
